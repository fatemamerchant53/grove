1
Go to linkedin.com and sign in.
2

3
Go to Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data
4
Select Connections only (faster — arrives in ~10 min), then click Request archive
5
LinkedIn emails you a download link. Open it, download the ZIP, and find Connections.csv inside.

Drop the CSV file below.
